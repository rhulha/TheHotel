what could be the "all is lost" moment?

Nate is caught trying to smuggle proof of the portal out of the Hotel. The staff, who are willing to kill to protect the secret, capture him and take him to the cellar. They plan to throw him into the portal as a sacrifice to keep it running.

The friend who warned him is the next sacrifice
The fellow employee who first warned Nate away from the room — and who became his ally — turns up dead, staged as an "accident" or a quiet "resignation." Nate realizes the Hotel already knows everything he's been doing, and that his curiosity got someone killed. He's alone, and it's his fault.

Nate finally smuggles out his evidence (footage, the ledger, a Universe1 object) only to find it's blank, decayed, or simply impossible on this side of the portal — the physics don't carry over.
