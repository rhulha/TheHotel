Nate gets hired at a very secretive Hotel.

He finds out that the Hotel has a portal in the cellar that leads to a utopian Universe2.

He finds out people use the portal to steal resources from Universe1 to support the lavish lifestyles of those in Universe2.

Nate wants to stop this, maybe even come up with a way to take all that was stolen back, but the powerful figures behind it all will stop at nothing to keep their utopia intact.

Nate discovers that his own counterpart in Universe2, Nate2, is complicit in maintaining this system and becomes the primary antagonist he must confront.

Nate has a plan to steal back the resources and expose the truth, but in the last moment he is captured by the bad guys.

Friends he made at the Hotel come to his rescue and free him.

He can't enact his plan to get back the resources anymore, but he can destroy the portal and end the exploitation.

Just before he does, Nate2 steps out of the portal and tries to persuade him not to do it, saying that the utopia is worth the cost and that Nate1 doesn't understand how good life is in Universe2. He tells him to come to Universe2.

He will get unfathomable wealth and power. He tells everything a man could be tempted by, maybe even a woman he loves comes through the portal and offers herself to him.

Nate has to make a choice. He can either destroy the portal and end the exploitation, or he can join his utopian self in Universe2 and live a life of luxury while the people in Universe1 continue to suffer.

He destroys the portal, but not before Nate2 tries to kill him. Nate1 manages to kill Nate2 in self-defense.

Last book sentence: "Nate had failed to take back everything the dark side had stolen, but he had succeeded in destroying the portal and giving the light side a fair chance to make it on their own. The cycle of exploitation was finally broken. Will they rise to the challenge? Time will tell."
