CHARACTERS - The Hotel

MAIN CHARACTER
--------------
Nate Jennings
- Protagonist, new employee at the Hotel
- Former front desk staff at Meridian Hotel (2 years), then concierge at Kensington (18 months)
- Grew up in the foster system
- Two years of community college, couldn't afford to finish
- No emergency contacts, no close family
- Adaptable, good at reading people, knows when to keep quiet
- Desperate for work - nearly broke, apartment lease ending

HOTEL STAFF
-----------
Michael Carver
- Manager of the Hotel
- Age 50-70, silver hair swept back, strong features, sharp eyes
- Wears tailored charcoal gray suits
- Speaks in measured tones, commands respect
- Emphasizes discretion above all else

David Wei
- Runs the day shift (6am - 2pm)
- Mid-twenties, sharp features, dark eyes
- Strict, efficient trainer
- Not warm but fair
- Punctuality and precision matter to him

Mrs. Chen
- Handles the night shift
- Forties, dark hair in severe bun
- Immaculate expensive suit
- Cool, professional demeanor

Rebecca Park
- Works the restaurant
- Mid-twenties, dark hair in a bun, sharp eyes
- Handles orders and manages the kitchen
- Demanding but competent
- Rarely smiles

Sophie Moreau
- Concierge and guest services
- Late twenties, auburn hair in loose ponytail, green eyes
- Friendly, helpful, warm
- Been at the Hotel for 3 years
- Friend of Elena (disappeared employee)
- Secretly investigating the Hotel's secrets with Marcus

Martin
- Works telecom/maintenance in the basement
- Fifties, thick glasses, large hands
- Handles secure equipment and bug detection
- Keeps to himself in his domain

Marcus (night shift)
- Works night shift (6pm - 2am)
- Late twenties, dark hair
- Five years at the Hotel
- Casually confident
- Also investigating the Hotel's secrets
- Friend of Elena

MENTIONED/DISAPPEARED
---------------------
Elena
- Former night shift employee
- Friend of Sophie and Marcus
- Saw something she wasn't supposed to see
- Disappeared - never showed up for shift, phone unanswered
- Apartment still had her stuff, car still parked outside
- Police refused to investigate

Marcus (Nate's friend)
- Works in hotel management (not at the Hotel)
- Recommended Nate for the job
- Gave Nate the address on a napkin at The Rusty Nail bar
